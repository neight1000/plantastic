About Plantastic

Plantastic is a collection of browser-based sound, synth, and UI experimentsâ€”each a standalone prototype focused on generative audio, ambient tools, MIDI control, and plant-inspired music synthesis. These tools are designed for rapid creative testing, music-making, and live demos, emphasizing clarity, playability, and transparent sound design. The open codebase encourages remixing and extension.

What You Can Do

  • Launch and test individual audio prototypes
  • Explore ambient tone and pattern generators
  • Control sound with MIDI and automation
  • Visualize live audio output with interactive, real-time waveforms
  • Compare user interfaces, presets, and effects across different versions

Audio & Visual Effects Highlights

  • Audio:
    • Delay & Echo: Adjustable by slider for time and intensity
    • Stereo Reverb: Deep, immersive sound with user control
    • Lowpass Filter & LFO: Animated timbre shaping, depth and rate adjustable
    • Master Volume, BPM, and Base Frequency: Full control over mix and tuning
    • Melodic Evolution: Notes/tones evolve using the Plant Pentatonic Scale or Camelot Wheel
    • Preset System: Instantly load unique effect settings, frequencies, and color mappings
    • Detuned Oscillators: 3+ detuned voices per tone create analog/chorus textures
    • Stereo Panning & Analog Envelope: Dynamic field and soft attack/release for organic sound
    • Optional Tape-Style Pitch Wobble & Tape Delay: Vintage character and movement
    • Oscillation Control: Adjust number of voices per tone
    • All audio tuned to plant harmonics/pentatonic scales for natural resonance
  • Visuals:
    • Animated Waveform: Real-time, gradient-enhanced, and beat-synced
    • Glow, echo/ghost trails, and beat-pulse background effects
    • Frequency Markers and Sinewave Grid overlays (toggleable)
    • Particles ("Butterflies"): Ambient, floating, peak-triggered, with customizable density and floatiness
    • Preset-Driven Gradients: Distinct evolving color themes for each preset
    • Camelot Wheel Key Feedback: Live display of key and color per tone
  • User Interface:
    • Unified, minimal black-and-white controls with live value display
    • Preset dropdown with instant color feedback
    • Fullscreen toggle (optional)

Concept & Philosophy

  • Rapid, intuitive creative testing for musicians and curious minds
  • Emphasis on transparent sound design and playability
  • Open code: remix, extend, and make it your own
